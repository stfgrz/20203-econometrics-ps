# SYLLABUS 2025/2026

**Massimiliano Marcellino**

The aim of this part of the course is to provide the students with an introduction to time series models and their use for forecasting. The emphasis will be more on the use of the techniques than on the underlying theory, but theoretical aspects related to model specification, estimation and evaluation will be also considered. All the models and techniques will be illustrated with empirical examples based on simulated and/or actual data, using the econometric software Eviews.
